Who We Are

God's Gift Charity Initiative is a community-based, Christ-centered organization located in Luweero district, central Uganda. Inspired by the suffering caused by the long-term effects of war in this region, we were founded to serve the most vulnerable members of the community — the orphans, the widows, the elderly, the women, and the forgotten. We believe that every person is a precious gift from God, created with dignity and purpose. Our work is to restore this dignity with love, care, and compassion.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To demonstrate love by empowering the poor and the needy through education, human rights advocacy, climate action, and community development.

Our Vision

A transformed community in Luweero where poverty is broken, rights are respected, communities are empowered, and every individual walks in the fullness of God's purpose and love.
