Restoring Hope in Luweero

Restoring Hope in Luweero

At God's Gifts Charity Initiative, our community outreach program exists to support families beyond. One such beneficiary is Calton Mukisa, a bright student at Gods Gifts Primary School in Primary One class who lives with his elderly grandmother after being orphaned. His grandmother is deeply needy and requires both empowerment and support to provide Calton with a stable home.However, her old age and constant illnesses worry her for the future of Calton and his siblings. Through our community outreach, we are making intentional efforts to help Calton's grandmother become self‑sustainable so she can afford a reasonable life and care for Calton and his siblings with dignity. This reflects our broader mission: empowering the community to break free from dependency and build lasting, self-sufficient livelihoods. But there are many more families like Calton's waiting for help. We call on you donors, partners, and well wishers to join us. Your support will enable us to reach more orphaned children and needy guardians, providing skills, resources, and hope. Together, we can transform more lives. Please give today.

One Child's Journey

One Child's Journey

At God's Gifts Charity Initiative, our Child Sponsorship Program is changing lives one child at a time. Meet Waswa and Nakato (twins) siblings in the Nursery section of God's Gifts Primary School. Their mother is deaf, a special need that sadly limits her ability to work, communicate freely, and provide adequate care for her young twins. Despite her deep love for them, she struggled daily to meet even their most basic needs, and the dream of sending Waswa and Nakato to school seemed impossible. When she courageously reached out to God's Gifts Charity Initiative, we immediately stepped in and enrolled Waswa and Nakato into our Child Sponsorship Program. Today, these twins attend school regularly, receive nutritious meals, and have all the learning materials they need. Their mother now watches with tearful joy as her children leave for school every morning with bright smiles on their faces that were once absent but now shine with hope and dignity. It is on this note that we make a heartfelt call to you individuals, families, and partners to join us in sustaining these smiles. Your support for our Child Sponsorship Program will help us reach more needy children like Waswa and Nakato. Sponsor a child today, and together, we will keep the light of education burning in the eyes of many more vulnerable children. Come and support our program because every child deserves a reason to smile.

Christian Ministry Impact

Christian Ministry Impact

Christian Ministry is one of the foundational pillars of all we do at God's Gifts Charity Initiative.The Name Gods Gifts charity initiative was a vision from our founder Mr.Wamala Tonny to extend suport to Gods love to his poeple.We therefore believe that serving the needy is not merely charity it is obedience to God's Word. The Bible commands us: "Defend the weak and the fatherless; uphold the cause of the poor and the oppressed" (Psalm 82:3). Proverbs 19:17 reminds us that "whoever is kind to the poor lends to the Lord, and He will reward them for what they have done." Guided by these truths, we base every outreach, every sponsorship, and every act of compassion on our faith in Jesus Christ, who Himself said, "Whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me" (Matthew 25:40). Furthermore, we intentionally nurture these children to walk in the light of the Christian faith, as the Lord commands us to do, raising them to know God's love and share it with others. Therefore, we call upon every Christian and Christian-based organization around the world to join us in this sacred effort. Together, with Christ at the center, we can make a lasting difference bringing hope to orphans, empowerment and smiles to the needy. We also invite you to support our broader missions at our school, in our communities, and at other schools through engaging in bible studies, street preaching, gospel music training and the construction of churches. Whether through prayer, volunteering, or financial partnership, your involvement helps us spread the Gospel while meeting physical needs. Partner with us today sponsor a child, support a mission, or help us build a church. Let our hands be His hands, and let every child and community we touch come to know the love of Jesus Christ.
